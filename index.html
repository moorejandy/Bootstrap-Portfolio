About Me

Hi, there! Thanks for stopping by.

I'm a recently certified Full-Stack Web Developer skilled in customer experience design, international development, and community mobilization, with demonstrated knowledge and success in data analysis, HTML 5, CSS, and javascript. Working on a vast array of unique projects, I’ve created life skills clubs for students in rural Namibia, established program requirements for the Peace Corps’ Community Health Program in that country, developed an employee rewards program for a large telecom company, and I am now increasing my capacity in building full-stack web applications.

I encompass strong intercultural, leadership, writing, and communication skills with a Full Stack Web Development Certificate from Penn LPS, and an MS in Applied Statistics. Whether it’s criminal justice reform, climate change, or closing the wealth gap, I want to jump in and use my growing skills as a developer, in conjunction with my ground-level experiences, to work towards building a better global future. My technical skills and education, alongside my emotional intelligence and proven ability to overcome adversity, make me a valuable contributor to any team.
